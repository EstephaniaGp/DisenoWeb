ESPAÑA-PUERTO DE NAVACERRADA

he Port of Navacerrada is a mountain pass that houses a ski resort and is located in the Sierra de Guadarrama (Central System). This separates the provinces of Segovia and Madrid, in the center of Spain. The north slope (the Segovian one) belongs to the municipality of Real Sitio de San Ildefonso and the south to Cercedilla.
This mountain pass and ski resort is very busy and has several hotels, apartments, hostels, restaurants and a ski school, it has beautiful streets and views
